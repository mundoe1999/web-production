Change Afoot

Professor Virginia Teller, who has served Hunter's Computer Science Department for more than three decades, has announced her stepping down from her position as Department Chair effective July1, 2013. Under her stewardship as Chair, the Department has developed a more reasearch-oriented approach to undergraduate education. She has also tirelessly sought to include underrepresented in the computational sciences.
